# Audit Scope and Goals

## Purpose
This document summarizes the audit scope and goals defined by management for the internal security audit of Botium Toys.

## Scope
The audit focuses on:
- Security controls
- User access and permissions
- Physical and digital assets
- Policies, procedures, and technologies
- Regulatory compliance

## Audit Goals
- Evaluate the current security posture
- Identify risks to critical assets
- Assess existing controls
- Identify potential compliance gaps
