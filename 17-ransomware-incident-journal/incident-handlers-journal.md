### **Who caused the incident?**
An organized group of unethical hackers known to target healthcare and transportation organizations.

### **What happened?**
Attackers deployed ransomware that encrypted critical systems and files after gaining initial access through a phishing email containing a malicious attachment.

### **When did the incident occur?**
Tuesday morning at approximately 9:00 a.m.

### **Where did the incident happen?**
At a small U.S.-based healthcare clinic specializing in primary care services.

### **Why did the incident happen?**
The attackers successfully exploited human vulnerability through phishing. An employee unknowingly downloaded a malicious attachment, allowing malware installation and enabling attackers to deploy ransomware across the network.
